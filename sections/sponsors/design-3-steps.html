Detailed Instructions

  1. Add a webpage block: Embeds → Sponsors
  2. If a Sponsors Embed does not exist, create a new Sponsors Embed:
    1. In the modal window that opens, click "Add New"
    2. A new window titled "Add Sponsor Embeds - Beta" will open
    3. Fill in the Name, Header, and other relevant fields
    4. Click the "Save Settings" button
    5. The page will refresh or update
    6. Check the "Include Your Business" option if needed
    7. Click the "Add a Source" button and add your sources
    8. Click the "Save Settings" button again
    9. Return to the page builder with the modal window still open
    10. Click "Save" in the modal window
    11. Click "Save Changes" on the page
    12. Click the orange settings button on the right. In the modal window that opens, choose Sponsors Embed from the dropdown list
    13. Click "Save" in the modal window
    14. Click "Save Changes" on the page
  3. Click the settings icon button on the left. In the "Set a Custom Class (Advanced)" modal window, add the following class: 
    special-styles-sponsors-3
  4. Click "Save" in the modal window
  5. In the top-right corner of the page, click "Advanced" options
  6. Copy the code snippet below and paste it into the "Page Head Injection" field, then click "Save Changes"
